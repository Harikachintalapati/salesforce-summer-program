Salesforce Summer Program - Day 1

1.What is CRM
CRM stands for Customer Relationship Management. It helps companies manage customer information, communication, and sales processes.

2.Why companies use Salesforce
Companies use Salesforce to store customer data, improve communication, automate tasks, and track sales opportunities.

3.Account
Account represents a company or organization.

4.Contact
Contact represents a person associated with an account.

5.Opportunity
Opportunity represents a possible business deal or process.

6.Business Flow
Lead → Contact → Opportunity → Customer

A lead is a person interested in a service or product. After collecting details, the lead becomes a contact. If there is a business possibility, it becomes an opportunity. When the deal is successful, they become a customer.

7.Real World Mapping (College Admission)

- Account = College
- Contact = Student
- Lead = Student Inquiry
- Opportunity = Admission Process
